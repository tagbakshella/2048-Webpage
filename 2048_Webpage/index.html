About 2048 Puzzle Game

2048 is a fun and addictive number puzzle game where you swipe tiles to combine numbers and reach the coveted 2048 tile. It’s simple to play but challenging to master!

Perfect for casual gaming!

Developed for Southern Leyte State University – Tomas Oppus, BSIT 2nd Year.

Key Features

  • Easy swipe controls for intuitive gameplay.
  • Minimalistic design for a clean and focused experience.
  • Dynamic animations and sound effects.
  • Leaderboard and achievements for competitive players.
  • Supports multiple devices with smooth performance.
